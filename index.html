About

Building at the intersection of AI & product

I'm a product leader who thrives at the intersection of cutting-edge AI and commercial impact. At Google, I spearhead global expansion of AI-powered advertising products — leading a team of 10 PMs from ideation to market, shipping products that have generated over $452M in annual recurring revenue.

Before Google, I spent five years at Deutsche Bank building AI-powered investing and risk management platforms that transformed how the firm served high-net-worth clients and managed enterprise risk.

I hold an MS in Information Technology & Analytics from Rutgers University and a B.E. in Computer Science from Goa College of Engineering. I speak at conferences, lead a media startup in India, and am always curious about what's next.
