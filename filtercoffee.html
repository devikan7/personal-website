The vision

India's retail investing boom has brought millions of new investors into the market — but most apps focus purely on transactions, leaving users without context, community, or clarity on what's driving markets.

FilterCoffee was born from a simple insight: investors don't just want to trade — they want to understand. We built a platform that blends curated financial news, community-driven discussions, and real-time market data into a single daily experience. Think of it as your morning market briefing — brewed fresh, served social.

My role & approach

As Head of Product, I own the full product lifecycle — from discovery and user research through design, development prioritization, and growth strategy. I work closely with a small, cross-functional team spanning engineering, design, and content.

We validated the core concept through a newsletter MVP, then expanded into a fully featured mobile app as engagement signals confirmed product-market fit. Every feature decision is grounded in user behavior data and direct community feedback.

What we built

☕

Daily Market Drops

Curated daily briefings that distill complex market movements into clear, digestible stories — from RBI policy to quarterly earnings surprises.

💬

Community Discussions

A social layer where investors share trades, debate theses, and react to news in real time — building conviction through collective intelligence.

📈

Real-Time Market Data

Live stock data, company profiles, and related company discovery — context alongside the conversation.

🌟

Content-Led Growth

High-quality financial content that drives subscribers without paid acquisition, growing to 15K+ organically.

Key learnings

Building FilterCoffee reinforced a conviction I carry into all my product work: the best products create a habit. By anchoring the experience around a daily ritual (the morning "drop"), we built retention into the core loop rather than relying on notifications or gamification.

The biggest unlock was treating content as product. Rather than bolting a content layer onto a data app, we designed the entire experience around the narrative — market data serves the story, not the other way around. This content-first approach has been the primary driver of our organic growth.
